Benefits of Reading

Reading helps with a lot but reading isn't everything. I mean sure it has its benefits but it can only take you so far.
I had a friend who read all day and night. He was hardworking always devoted to his work. We all knew his future was safe.
Sorry to burst your bubble but he got hit by a bus and now he's paralyzed,poor soul
Moral of the story,Reading will help you reach where you want but it can never stop a bus from hitting you.
